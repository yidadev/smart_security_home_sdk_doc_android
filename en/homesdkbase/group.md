## Group

### 1.1 Group overview

Tuya Cloud supports a group management system: you can create groups, modify group names, manage group devices, manage multiple devices through groups, and disband groups. Tuya Smart provides some interfaces for device group control.[Group function overview](<https://tuyainc.github.io/tuyasmart_home_android_sdk_doc/en/resource/Group.html#functional-overview>)
